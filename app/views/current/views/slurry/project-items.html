Which items available through grant funding does the project need?

Slurry and digestate management equipment

All slurry and digestate management equipment must include:
  • inline nutrient sensors
  • flow meters
  • adjustable flow control valves
  • GPS receiver
  • in-cab display/control unit

Low emission precision application equipment

Umbilical systems and tankers

Must include a:
  • pump
  • macerator
  • reeler

Slurry/digestate storage bags

Storage bags provide additional storage capacity over and above the statutory slurry storage requirement.
May include site preparation and associated pumps and stirrers.

Advanced processing equipment

Acidification

Must include a:
  • mixing tank
  • acid storage
  • dosing equipment
