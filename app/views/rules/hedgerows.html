You must take all reasonable steps to keep a green cover on land within 2 metres of the centre of a hedgerow. You must not cultivate or apply fertilisers or pesticides to land within 2 metres of the centre of a hedgerow. Find out more.

Apply for a hedgerow and boundaries grant

The Hedgerow and Boundaries standalone capital grant is available to help restore existing farm boundaries and bring environmental and landscape benefits to your land.

Find out more by reading the:

  • hedgerows and boundaries grant manual 2020 – covers eligibility, who can apply and when and how to apply
  • options, supplements and capital items 2020 – covers information on grant eligibility and requirements for hedgerow laying, coppicing, gapping-up, planting new hedges and supplements – casting up, substantial pre-work, top binding and staking

See Countryside Stewardship grant finder.
