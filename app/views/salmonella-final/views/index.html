Salmonella - test your laying hens Changes made based on user research:
  • included a legal requirements summary
    This is written in bullets. Farmers can scan this one section and gain an understanding of the rules. In UR this worked and allowed users to identify what they legally have to do quickly
  • made it clearer what the farmer has to do
    Structured the page to put information in the order the farmer has to do it
  • made better use of the contents
    Users don't read the full page, they use the contents to jump to the section they need. Rewritten h2s to guide users through the process of testing for salmonella.
  • limited the amount of information in a section
    We had a "what you must do" section before that was too long, users got lost and had to do a lot of scrolling
