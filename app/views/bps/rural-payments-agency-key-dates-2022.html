Basic Payment Scheme

  • 1 January 2022 – BPS 2022 scheme year begins
  • 18 March 2022 – BPS application window opens
  • 17 May 2022 (midnight) – deadline for BPS 2022 applications without penalty (including Young and New Farmer applications). Deadline to transfer entitlements and to have land at customer’s disposal
  • 11 June 2022 (midnight) – deadline for late BPS 2022 applications (with penalty) and for making certain changes without penalty to a BPS application already submitted by midnight on 17 May
  • 31 December 2022 – BPS 2022 scheme year ends
  • 1 December 2022 to 30 June 2023 – RPA makes BPS 2022 payments

Countryside Stewardship and Environmental Stewardship claims

  • 18 March 2022 – opening of CS and ES revenue claims
  • 17 May 2022 (midnight) – deadline for CS and ES revenue claims without reduction. Deadline to transfer entitlements and to have land at customer’s disposal
  • 31 May 2022 (midnight) – deadline for making certain changes to a CS revenue claim that was submitted before midnight on 17 May without receiving a reduction
  • 11 June 2022 (midnight) – deadline for late CS and ES revenue claims (with reduction)
  • 1 December 2022 to 30 June 2023 – RPA makes CS and ES 2022 payments
  • ES Higher Level Stewardship (HLS) and CS capital claims can be submitted all year

Countryside Stewardship applications

9 February 2022 – applications open for Higher Tier, Mid Tier and Wildlife Offers for agreements starting on 1 January 2023, and for Capital Grants agreements starting in 2022.

The new Woodland Creation and Maintenance Grant also replaces the Woodland Creation Grant from that date.

Deadline for requesting application packs

  • 31 March 2022 – Higher Tier (paper and online)

  • 28 May 2022 – Mid Tier and Wildlife Offers (paper)

  • 16 July 2022 – Mid Tier (online)

Online applicants for Wildlife Offers do not need to request an application pack.

Deadline for submitting your application for a 2023 agreement

  • 30 April 2022 – Higher Tier and Capital Grants

  • 30 July 2022 – Mid Tier and Wildlife Offers

Agreement offers sent out

  • From July 2022 – Capital Grants
  • From September 2022 – Higher Tier, Mid Tier and Wildlife Offers

Open all year for applications

  • Woodland Tree Health applications
  • Woodland Management Plan applications
  • Woodland Creation applications – up to 8 February 2022 only
  • Woodland Creation and Maintenance Grant applications – from 9 February 2022
