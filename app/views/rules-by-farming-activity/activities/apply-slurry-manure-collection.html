Apply manure, slurry or fertiliser

Rules farmers and land managers must follow when they apply manure, slurry or fertiliser to land.

You must avoid causing pollution from your farming activities.

These are legal requirements to protect the environment. The Environment Agency enforces the rules around applying manure, slurry and fertiliser. They may offer advice and instructions to change how you deal with these. You can be fined or prosecuted if you break the law.

Plan before applying manure or slurry

You must plan before you apply manure, slurry or fertiliser so that you meet soil and crop need. Also consider the risks of pollution from the land where you intend to apply it.

Those in nitrate vulnerable zones, areas identified as at risk from nitrate pollution from agriculture, must follow extra rules.
