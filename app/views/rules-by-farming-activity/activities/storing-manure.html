Storing manure

Find out the rules about storing manure on your farm.

Contents

  3. Source legislation

Farmyard manure (FYM) can be stored in a temporary field heap provided that it is:

  • ‘stackable’ and does not slump
  • stored at least 10m from any inland freshwaters or coastal waters
  • 50m from any spring, well or borehole
  • not stored anywhere where it may cause a significant risk of diffuse pollution

FYM can be stored in a yard on an impermeable surface provided that any runoff is collected and stored as slurry. You must take into account the risk of runoff when deciding where to store manure.

Using a mechanical slurry separator

If you use a mechanical slurry separator you need to store and spread the separated liquid as slurry.

Who deals with odour or flies from a manure heap

Odour or flies from a field heap would be considered a statutory nuisance. This is regulated by the local council.

Source legislation

These rules are from:

  • The Reduction and Prevention of Agricultural Diffuse Pollution (England) Regulations 2018
