You must avoid causing pollution from your farming activities.

These are legal requirements to protect the environment. The Environment Agency enforces the rules around slurry. They may offer advice and instructions to change how you deal with slurry. You can be fined or prosecuted if you break the law.

Storing manure

You can store manure in a temporary field heap if it is ‘stackable’ and does not slump.
