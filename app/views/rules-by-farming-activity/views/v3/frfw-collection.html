Managing livestock and feeders to prevent diffuse pollution

Details

Farmers must follow the rules to prevent soil, sediment, organic manure, manufactured fertiliser, or any substances found within them from getting into inland or coastal waters.

You must follow these rules when:

  • planning applications of organic manure and manufactured fertiliser
  • storing organic manure
  • applying organic manure
  • applying manufactured fertiliser
  • doing any land management and cultivation activities
  • managing livestock and locating their feeders

The Environment Agency (EA) will give you guidance so that you can meet the rules. They may inspect your farm to help you identify changes you need to make and agree a timescale for you to make them.

Find out more about how the Environment Agency applies the farming rules for water.

For advice contact the Environment Agency or Farming Advice Service.
