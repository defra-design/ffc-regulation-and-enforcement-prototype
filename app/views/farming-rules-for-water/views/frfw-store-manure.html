Legal requirements

You must not store organic manure:

  • within 10m of inland freshwaters or coastal waters
  • where risk factors like soil condition, nearby waterways or drains mean thereâ€™s a significant risk of pollution
  • within 50m of a spring, well or borehole

Best practice when you chose a site for manure storage

Before you make a manure store, check if there is a significant risk of pollution from the location.

There are significant risks of runoff and soil erosion from factors like the:

  • angle of slopes, in particular if the angle is greater than 12 degrees
  • amount of ground cover
  • soil type and condition
  • presence and condition of land drains

Check the area around stored organic manures and identify and block any potential pathways where runoff could flow into water.

On a farm map or plan, record the locations of:

  • waterways, field drains, springs, wells and boreholes
  • manure stores
